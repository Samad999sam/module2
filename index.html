Chawarma




Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish featuring thinly sliced, seasoned meat (often lamb, chicken, or beef) that is traditionally cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The flavorful meat is typically served in a flatbread, accompanied by various condiments like tahini sauce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions. Shawarma is known for its delicious combination of tender, spiced meat and fresh toppings.

French Tacos




French Tacos are a unique and popular fast-food item in France, characterized by a large tortilla filled with grilled meat (such as chicken or beef), French fries, cheese, and various sauces, creating a fusion of Mexican and French flavors.

Pizza




Pizza is an iconic Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients like vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is typically baked in an oven, resulting in a savory and customizable culinary delight enjoyed worldwide.
